SMS Opt-In

Manage your text message preferences with Drop The Strap Marketing.

What You're Signing Up For

Drop The Strap Marketing offers two types of SMS messages. You can opt into either or both independently.

Transactional Messages

Service-related messages including appointment confirmations, project updates, report delivery notifications, and other communications directly related to services you've purchased or inquired about.

Marketing Messages

Promotional messages including special offers, new service announcements, and search marketing insights from Drop The Strap Marketing.

Opt-In Form

You must select at least one message type. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Important Information

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies. Transactional messages are sent only in relation to services you've purchased or inquired about. Marketing messages are limited and sent only when we have something relevant to share.

Opt-Out: You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. You can also contact us at scott@creatingvaluellc.com to update your preferences.

Help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at scott@creatingvaluellc.com.

Message & Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply. Check with your carrier for details.

Carriers Supported: Compatible with all major US carriers.

Privacy: Your phone number and consent preferences are stored securely. We do not share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. See our full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Business Identity: Drop The Strap Marketing is a project brand of Creating Value LLC, 13 S Prescott St, Unit 11013, Memphis, TN 38111.
